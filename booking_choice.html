Common Questions

Can I switch from single sessions to a package later?
Absolutely! Many families start with single sessions to experience our approach, then upgrade to packages for the savings. You can switch anytime.
What's your cancellation policy?
Sessions must be rescheduled or cancelled at least 24 hours in advance. For emergencies or illness, please text or email immediately. No refunds for no-shows without notice.
How do payment plans work for packages?
Packages can be paid in full or through weekly installments. For example, a $850 package can be paid as $85/week for 10 weeks. Installments are automatically charged to your card.
What if I'm outside your service area?
We offer virtual sessions to families anywhere! If you're beyond our 60-minute drive radius, virtual sessions provide the same quality support without travel limitations.
